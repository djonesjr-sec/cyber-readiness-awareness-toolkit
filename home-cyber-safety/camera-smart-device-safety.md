## Recommended Home Network Setup

A safer home setup usually separates devices into different networks. On many home routers, these appear as different Wi-Fi names.

### 1. Main Network
Use this for trusted personal devices such as:
- phones
- laptops
- tablets
- work computers

### 2. Smart Device / IoT Network
Use this for internet-connected smart devices such as:
- security cameras
- video doorbells
- smart TVs
- thermostats
- smart plugs
- voice assistants

### 3. Guest Network
Use this for visitors and temporary devices.

## Why This Helps

The goal is to separate personal devices, smart devices, and guest devices.

If a smart device or visitor’s device is compromised, such as through malware, stolen passwords, or unauthorized access, separation helps reduce the chance of it reaching your personal devices, work computers, or private files.
